Research

I'm currently a student researcher in Berkeley Artificial Intelligence Research Lab (BAIR) advised by Prof. Yi Ma and in FHL Vive Center for Enhanced Reality's OpenARK Project advised by Dr. Allen Yang. I also work closely with Xili Dai from Prof. Heung-Yeung Shum's lab in HKUST(GZ). My research interest lies in interpretability of deep learning, sparse coding, generative models, and representation learning. I'm also interested in using deep learning to understand visual perception in primates and work with Prof. Doris Tsao on interpreting neural data with generative models.
